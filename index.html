A Short Summary

Disney, a global leader in entertainment, has enchanted audiences with its magical stories and experiences for decades. Founded by Walt Disney, the company is renowned for its classic animated films, innovative media ventures, and world-famous theme parks. Disney’s characters, from Mickey Mouse to Elsa, have become cultural icons, while its imaginative worlds continue to inspire joy and wonder in families around the globe. Disney’s legacy of creativity remains unmatched.
